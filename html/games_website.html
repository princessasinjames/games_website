ðŸ›¸ Minecraft

Minecraft is a game where you can try to survive in a world with/without monsters, or build infinitely in a creative world where you cannot die of natural causes. There are many animals in Minecraft which can be helpful for survival, especially if you create a farm. This is an interesting way to survive in the game, since alternatives are growing crops for days, fishing after crafting/finding a fishing rod, eating rotten flesh from zombies which come out at night, or trying to find random edible food items (i.e. sweet berries and mushrooms). Building is another fun part of the game, since there are so many things you can build with and many different kinds of wood, including cherry wood, which is adorably pink.
